About Me

Artist, Thinker, Developer

I am Web Developer/Designer

Hi my name is Vanessa and I currently live in Vancouver, BC Canada. I am a self-taught designer and developer with a degree in Electrical Engineering at University of British Columbia. My absolute biggest passion is UX design, but I also love to cook, bake, and try out local restaurants (yes, all about food!)

My passion for User Experience design started when I designed and wireframed my own E-Commerce website in high school. I spent hours on sketching my ideas to not only design an aesthetically pleasing website but also a user-centered one. I instantly fell in love with the power and thrill of designing solutions to solve problems for the user or improve some aspect of the user's life.
